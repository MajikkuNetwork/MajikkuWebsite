Welcome to Majikku

The Void-Anchor Alliance

Welcome to Majikku, a Hytale server built on a passion for connection and creativity. Our mission is simple but powerful: to build a welcoming community where players want to hang out and can make friends for a lifetime. We believe a server is defined by its people, which is why we are dedicated to fostering an inclusive environment where everyone—from casual players to hardcore adventurers—feels right at home. This isn't just a game network; it's a place to belong. As a passion project driven by a dedicated team of volunteers, Majikku is constantly evolving to become a magical world that will stand the test of time. This website serves as your gateway to our universe, offering the latest news, lore updates, and a direct line to our team. Whether you are here to catch up on announcements or apply to help us build this dream, we invite you to join us and leave your mark on our growing story.
