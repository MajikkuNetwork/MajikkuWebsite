Server Rules

Law & Order

01

Respect

Treat all players and staff with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and toxicity are strictly prohibited.

02

No Cheating

Hacked clients, x-ray, and macros that give an unfair advantage are not allowed.

03

Griefing

Do not destroy other players' builds. If it isn't yours, don't touch it unless given permission.

04

Advertising

Do not advertise other servers or websites in chat or DMs.

05

Staff Discretion

Staff decisions are final. If you disagree, use the /appeal or /report systems, do not argue in global chat.
