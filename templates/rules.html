Server Rules

Law & Order

01

Respect

Treat every member with kindness. We have zero tolerance for harassment, toxicity, or bullying.

02

No Hate Speech

Zero tolerance for slurs or discriminatory language based on race, religion, gender, or orientation.

03

No Sexual References

This is a PG-13 server. No NSFW content, erotica, or explicit roleplay in public chat or DMs.

04

No Spamming

Do not flood the chat, spam commands, or use excessive caps/characters.

05

No Griefing

Respect others' builds. Destroying or stealing from other players without permission is prohibited.

06

Alternate Accounts

Limit of 1 alternate account per player. Using alts to bypass bans or hoard resources is forbidden.

07

No Advertising

Do not post IP addresses, invites, or links to other servers in chat or DMs.

08

No Hacking

Usage of hacked clients, cheat engines, or macros (e.g., Killaura, Fly, Speed) is strictly banned.

09

Unapproved Mods

Only aesthetic mods (shaders) are allowed. Mods giving unfair data (X-Ray, Radar) are banned.

10

No Exploits

Report bugs immediately. Exploiting glitches or duping items for personal gain is prohibited.

11

No Excessive Swearing

Keep swearing to a minimum. Aggressive vulgarity directed at others is not allowed.

12

No Sensitive Topics

Keep chat lighthearted. Avoid controversial topics like politics, religion, or trauma dumping.

13

Appropriate Content

Usernames and skins must be PG-13. No hate symbols or nudity are permitted.

14

Staff Discretion

Staff decisions are final. We reserve the right to punish disruptive behavior not explicitly listed here.
